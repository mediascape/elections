Module: mediascape/AdaptationToolkit/adaptation/UIAdaptation/UIAdaptation

UserInterfaceAdaptationEngine module is the responsible of render webcomponents the proper way taking in account a number of webcompoents and agents context. It receives an input of N webcomponents to show, and depends of the context and configuration, it renders some template or other template. It is ready to receives template changes remotely. Templates are based on layoutConstructor
