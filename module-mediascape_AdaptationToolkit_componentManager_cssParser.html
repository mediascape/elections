Module: mediascape/AdaptationToolkit/componentManager/cssParser

The main goal of the cssParser module is to facilitate the parsing content related with the css styleSheets. Mediascape uses a custom Properties of css and also it manipulates the styles of webcomponents . That is the main reason why Mediascape has its own parser of css. This is part of componentManager, lacks of utility out of mediascape.
