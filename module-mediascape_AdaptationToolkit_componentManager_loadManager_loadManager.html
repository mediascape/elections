Module: mediascape/AdaptationToolkit/componentManager/loadManager/loadManager

This module help to isolate the problem of load/unload resources related with the webcompoenents. The main goal is to facilitate a simple way to achieve a better way to unload/load resources related content. On this early version only take cares about multimedia resources. But it is ready to do it with other resources.
